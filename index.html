Frequently Asked Questions


      

        What is Price Pilot?
        
Price Pilot is an AI-powered grocery price comparison tool. You enter the items you need and the stores near you, and we analyze which store (or combination of two stores) gives you the best deal.

      


      

        How accurate are the prices?
        
Price Pilot uses AI to look up current prices from store websites based on your zip code. While we aim for accuracy, prices may vary by location and availability. The analysis will always note when a price is estimated vs. sourced from the store directly.

      


      

        Why do I need a zip code?
        
Many stores have region-specific pricing. Your zip code helps us provide prices that are relevant to your local area rather than national averages.

      


      

        Do I need an account?
        
No! You can use Price Pilot as a guest with full functionality. However, creating a free account lets you save and revisit your past comparisons anytime.

      


      

        What is the "Best Store Pair" feature?
        
If splitting your shopping list across two stores saves money compared to buying everything at one store, we'll show you exactly which items to buy where. If a single store is already the cheapest, we'll tell you that instead.

      


      

        Which stores are supported?
        
You can enter any US retail or grocery store. Popular ones include Walmart, Target, Costco, Kroger, Whole Foods, Aldi, Trader Joe's, Safeway, and Amazon Fresh. The AI will do its best to find prices for any store you enter.

      


      

        Is my data private?
        
Yes. Your comparisons are only visible to you when logged in. We don't sell or share your data. Guest sessions are not stored at all.

      

    


    
    

      
About Price Pilot


      

        

          
Our Mission

          
Grocery shopping shouldn't require a spreadsheet. Price Pilot was built to make smart shopping effortless. We believe everyone deserves to save money without spending hours comparing prices across store websites.

        


        

          
How It Works

          
Price Pilot combines AI analysis with real store pricing data. When you submit a comparison, our system checks each store's website for current prices in your area, computes the totals, and determines whether one store or a two-store split gives you the best value.

        


        

          
Technology

          
Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on the frontend, with Supabase for authentication and data storage, and Anthropic's Claude AI for intelligent price analysis and comparison.

        


        

          
The Team

          
Price Pilot was created at NTHSHack as a tool to solve a real everyday problem: figuring out where to shop to save the most money.
